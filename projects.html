Bookish — Personal Reading Tracker

• Next.js 15 (App Router) • React 19 • TypeScript • Supabase (Auth & Postgres + RLS) • Tailwind CSS v4 • Vercel •

Built a reading tracker designed with personality and atmosphere — not just a spreadsheet with a login screen, but a tool that matches how people actually read.
- Track reading progress with page history, start/finish dates, and status timelines.
- Write spoiler-safe notes tied to your current page — hidden until you read past them.
- Organize books into series and monitor progress across multi-book arcs.
- Search and add books by title, author, or ISBN via Google Books integration with bulk scan mode.
- 12 genre-inspired visual themes that transform the entire interface, plus solid color modes for distraction-free reading.
- Virtual bookshelves with customizable spine colors and flexible library views with sorting and filtering.
- Mark books as Did Not Finish with optional context.
